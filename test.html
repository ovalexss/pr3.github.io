How much does it cost to open and manage a company in Estonia?
Opening a company in Estonia with Enty costs €350 with e-Residency and €790 through a notary (the gov fee of €290 is included in both prices). Please notice that the issuance of an e-Residency card itself costs €120 (charged by the Estonian government).

Assistance with bank account opening is included in our subscriptions. However, some banks might charge you extra (from €0 to €100) for the processing of your application. At the same time, please keep in mind that some corporate services from the side of the government might require gov fees. That’s all for the one-time costs for company opening.

There are also recurring costs — firstly, each company is required to have a legal address and a contact person in Estonia (both are included in our subscriptions).
Other recurring costs are quite individual.

Enty allows you to take care of accounting, invoicing, and other corporate issues. Our subscriptions start from just €25.
